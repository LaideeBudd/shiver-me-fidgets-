About Skully the Pirate
and his gang of scallywags

Skully the Pirate is a multi-purpose pirate fidget toy. He can stretch; he can fidget; he can fly. Skully pirates a ship called The Fiddle-Fidget Frigate.

Skully and his buddies, Skurvy Shark, North Star, and Stretchy Skeleton, are out on the hunt for some sunken treasure.

Follow the treasure map to solve the riddles, solve the passkey, and discover the sunken treasure.
